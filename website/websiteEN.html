About us



Our origin

Alpenwasser stands for purity, regionality, and sustainability. Our spring water originates high up in the Austrian Alps,
where untouched nature, crystal-clear air, and decades of tranquility create the basis for its exceptional quality.
Since our founding in 2012, we have been committed to preserving this natural gift responsibly and refining it with the utmost care.



Our philosophy

For us, water is more than just a drink; it is a part of our quality of life. That's why we place great importance on transparency, environmental awareness, and fair partnerships. Every bottle of Alpenwasser tells a story of purity,
trust, and regional responsibility. We work closely with local communities, environmentalists, and research institutions
to ensure that our actions remain in harmony with people and nature.



Sustainability as a commitment

From resource-saving filling to fully recyclable packaging: sustainability is not a trend, but our daily commitment. Our production site is powered entirely by renewable energy, and we keep our carbon footprint to a minimum thanks to short transport routes.



Our promise

Those who drink Alpenwasser make a conscious decision in favor of quality, origin, and trust. We stand for authentic values, genuine regionality, and a respectful approach to our environment. Because only if we act mindfully today will the source of our future remain clear and vibrant.
